Contribute

What is Physio-pedia?

Physio-pedia is a registered charity and relies on financial and content contributions to support this professional online resource. Developing and updating content regularly are the main ways to contribute.

Benefits to contributor

Being a contributor gives you the opportunity to contribute to keep the physiotherapy / physical therapy community up to date and aware of all the current concepts in the world of Physiotherapy! By taking ownership of just one page, that interests you, you can reach millions of people. Wouldn't that be amazing?

How to Contribute?

Physio-pedia relies on the professional community to develop and maintain the Physiopedia content. If you are a physiotherapist or physical therapist any where in the world you can make edits to the Physiopedia content. If you see something on the Physiopedia website that needs correcting or updating, from a simple spelling mistake to an outdated technique, we invite you to update it.

That's a lot of pages to monitor and update, but imagine if every page in Physiopedia had an owner, always making sure the content was up to date and awesome. The one page project gives you the opportunity to contribute to keeping the physiotherapy / physical therapy community up to date and aware of all the current concepts in the world of Physiotherapy! By taking ownership of just one page, that interests you, you can reach millions of people.
